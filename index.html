We've Got A new Look!

iSpot updated our user experience in May of 2018 which made navigation, search, and usability easier and provided our clients with the option to opt-in to the new interface. On July 30, 2018, iSpot will be switching all remaining users to the new user experience. Benefits include:

Global View

A new Global View which enables you to discover trends in the industries, brands, networks, spots and shows you have access to.

My Brand

A new My Brand page which enables you to specify the primary brand that appears on your personalized My Brand page.

Easy Access

The iSpot products you have access to are now front and center in the navigation giving you faster access to the information you need.

After we switch you to the new interface you will be able to toggle between the new and classic view thriough August 15, 2018. If you want to switch to the new user experience now it takes less than a minute to make the change.

If you take no action, you'll be switched automatically to the new interface on July 30, 2018. For more information about the new iSpot experience, you can download this overview document or contact your Customer Success Manager.
